# perosnaltracker
Accompanied by a slideshow background, personal_tracker helps you keep track of your goals every step of the way.
FEATURES INCLUDE
1. A simple and stylish dashboard that gives you easy access to all the features below.
2.A simple to-do list that can be updated complete when done with the named task.
3.A simple entry for your long-term and short-term goals. Keeps track of date of entry and date to be accomplished.
4.Comes with a timetable where you can plan your day's activities as per your goal.
5.It also comes with a platform where you can enter all lessons you have learnt.
